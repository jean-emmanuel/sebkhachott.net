Not your language ?

The french crowdfunding tool we use is not translated yet, so here are some notes to help you.

Step 1 - Amount

  1. Choose a compensation or enter the amount you wanna give (last item)
  2. Check the box if you don't want your name to appear on the crowdfunding page
  3. Click on "Suivant" to continue

Step 2 - Address

  1. You don't care about the first two lines unless you are a company.

    Monsieur/Madame = Mr./Mrs.
    Prénom = First name
    Nom = Last name
    Adresse = Address
    Ville = City
    Code postal = Zip code
    Pays = Country

    (You can leave a public note in the text area if you want)
  2. Click on "Suivant" to continue

Step 3 - Summary

  1. The company (helloasso) that hosts our crowdfunding does not take any percentage on transactions, but you can leave a tip for them. Check the box if you don't want to.
  2. Récapitulatif = Summary
  3. Click on "Suivant" to continue
